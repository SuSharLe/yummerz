Ingredients

  • Vanilla ice cream (or your preferred flavor)
  • Cornflakes or crushed graham crackers
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour and Butter
  • Oil
  • Honey or chocolate syrup

Instructions

  • Form Ice Cream Balls: Scoop the ice cream into balls and freeze them for at least 2-3 hours.
  • Prepare Coating: Crush cornflakes and mix with cinnamon and sugar.
  • Coat the Ice Cream: Dip the frozen ice cream balls in the crushed cornflakes mixture, then in an egg wash, and coat again with cornflakes.
  • Fry: Quickly fry in hot oil for about 20-30 seconds until golden brown.
  • Serve: Drizzle with honey or chocolate syrup.

Reminders

  • Work Quickly: When frying, don't let the ice cream sit in the batter or coating for too long.
  • Oil Temperature: Ensure the oil is at 375°F (190°C) before frying.
  • Small Batches: Fry the ice cream balls in small batches to avoid overcrowding the pan.
  • Drain Well: After frying, drain the ice cream balls on paper towels to remove excess oil.
