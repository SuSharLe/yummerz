Ingredients


  • Ground beef (80% lean)
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, pickles (optional)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo)

Instructions


  • Prepare patties: Season ground beef with salt and pepper, shape into patties.
  • Cook patties: Grill or pan-fry patties on medium heat for 3-4 minutes per side.
  • Toast buns: Lightly toast burger buns on the grill or in a pan.
  • Assemble: Place cooked patty on the bottom bun, add cheese (optional), then top with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and condiments.
  • Serve: Add the top bun and serve hot.

Reminders


  • Meat quality: Use good quality, fresh ground beef (around 80% lean) for juicy, flavorful burgers.
  • Don't overwork the meat: When forming the patties, handle the meat gently to keep them tender.
  • Indent the center: Make a small indent in the center of the patty to prevent it from puffing up while cooking.
  • Cooking temperature: Avoid pressing the patty while cooking, as it squeezes out the juices.
  • Cheese melting: Add cheese during the last minute of cooking so it melts perfectly.
  • Customizing toppings: Feel free to add onions, bacon, or any other toppings you like for extra
