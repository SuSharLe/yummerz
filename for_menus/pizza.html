Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • Olive oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni, sausage, or any meats

Instructions

  • Prepare the Dough: Mix yeast, warm water, sugar, and salt. Let it sit for a few minutes until it gets foamy.
  • Prepare the Sauce: Heat olive oil, and sauté garlic for a minute. Add tomato sauce, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  • Assemble the Pizza: Roll out the dough to your desired size and thickness.
  • Bake: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden

Reminders

  • Stretch, Don’t Roll the Dough: When shaping your pizza, stretch the dough by hand instead of using a rolling pin.
  • Preheat Your Oven: Preheat your oven to its highest setting (around 475°F or 245°C).
  • Pre-Cook Certain Toppings: Some toppings, like mushrooms, onions, or sausage must be pre-cooked in order to avoid soggy pizza.
  • Let It Rest: After baking, let your pizza rest for a couple of minutes before slicing.
